What Is This?

This is a population index. Each column is a collection of species, and each row is a year.

Index is just a way of saying it's not the exact number of birds, it's just a proportion relative to some agreed amount (the population when first recorded, in this case).

Questions

  1. What is the average index from 2013?
  2. What are the best and worst performing species groups in 2013?
  3. Can you draw a bar chart of 2013?
